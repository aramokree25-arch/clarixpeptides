Long-Term Storage

Store lyophilised powder at −20°C. Stable for up to 24 months under these conditions. Protect from light and moisture at all times.

Short-Term Storage

Store at 2–8°C for short-term use up to 3 months. Keep sealed and away from humidity. Avoid repeated temperature fluctuations.

After Reconstitution

Once reconstituted with sterile or bacteriostatic water, refrigerate at 2–8°C and use within 28 days. Do not freeze reconstituted solution. For in-vitro research use only.

Freeze-Thaw Cycles

Avoid repeated freeze-thaw cycles as this may degrade peptide integrity. Aliquot into single-use volumes before freezing where possible.
